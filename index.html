We are taking every precautionary measure we can to limit any potential exposure to COVID-19. For the safety of our employees, patients and their families, we are limiting traffic in our office.

We are asking that patients be accompanied BY ONE HEALTHY PARENT/ ADULT. Other adults or sick children not being seen should stay home if at all possible.

Our waiting room will not be utilized as a means to wait for an exam room. Aquino Pediatrics will utilize "curbside check in" effective immediately. All patients should wait in car and call our office upon your arrival.

Once an exam room becomes available, our staff will call you or come to your car.

If your child is sick, park closest to our front door. If your child is well, please park either in the back or on the left side of our lot as you pull in.

If you are coming to our office to pick up a prescription or paperwork, please call our office once you arrive and someone will bring out to you.

Thank you for your attention and understanding.

Wash your hands often, cover your cough and sneeze, avoid touching your face and eyes.
