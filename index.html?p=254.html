Management Team

Stephanie, Practice Administrator

Stephanie has been with the practice since Dr. Barbara Aquino was with her group practice as Alpha Medical in September 1996. She received her Bachelors of Science degree in Health Care Management in December 1996 from Austin Peay State University. Stephanie takes great pride in overseeing all of the business aspects of the practice. She is certified in CPR. Stephanie is a member of the local chapter of CMGMA and the TMGMA. She is married with three boys. She is involved with her boys activities by serving on the board at the little league where they play and also serving as a treasurer for their cub scout pack. She spends her time volunteering at their school when she can, at the ball park, working out, running, gardening and simply being with her family and friends.
